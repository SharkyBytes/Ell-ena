# Ell-ena Logo

A modern and minimal logo for **Ell-ena**, an AI-powered teammate that makes managing work effortless.

## Design Concept
- **Intelligence & AI:** The symbol reflects neural connections and automation.
- **Simplicity:** Clean, minimal design for modern UI and documentation.
- **Trust & Collaboration:** Balanced lines evoke a reliable teammate.
